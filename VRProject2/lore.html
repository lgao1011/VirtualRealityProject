Game Lore

You awaken in the middle of an endless desert with no memory of how you arrived. Ancient portals scattered throughout the land seem to be the only way out. However, the desert is not empty. Strange enemies guard the paths and the environment itself is a maze designed to trap travelers forever.

To escape, you must navigate the dunes, survive enemy attacks, and discover the hidden portals that lead deeper into this mysterious world.

Stage 1: The Dune Maze

You begin in a massive maze made of towering sand dunes. Visibility is limited and paths twist in every direction. Somewhere within the maze is a portal that will lead you to the next area.

  • Navigate through the maze
  • Defeat enemies blocking your path
  • Locate the hidden portal to advance

Stage 2: The Mountain Cave

After escaping the dunes, you arrive inside a rocky cave hidden within the mountains. The portal here is locked and will not open until you activate a hidden button somewhere inside the cave.

  • Search the cave to find the hidden button
  • Enemies will continuously respawn
  • Enemies will chase you until you escape through the portal

Objectives

  1. Navigate the giant dune maze
  2. Defeat enemies guarding the paths
  3. Find the portal hidden in the dunes
  4. Search the cave for the hidden button
  5. Activate the portal and escape
