A Dune's Adventure

Welcome to the desert. Explore the dunes and escape the mysterious world.

Tips For Players

  • Use sprint to escape enemies when you are overwhelmed.
  • Parrying enemy attacks can give you an opening to counterattack.
  • In the dune maze, try to remember landmarks so you don't get lost.
  • In the cave stage, enemies respawn endlessly, so focus on finding the button quickly.
